Movies:

Harold and Maude
Year: 1971
Director: Hal Ashby

 The costumes, the music, the story. It all warms my heart in a strange way. I relate to Harold's cynicism but also am remininded to look at the beauty in everything, like Maude. Watched this for the first time at the MoMA.

Fight Club
Year: 1999
Director: David Fincher

 This movie postitioned itself in my life as the start line to a subtle type of anarchy in me. I rewatch this often, finding more inisight anger towards capitalism, consumerism and the American Dream each time.

Everything Everywhere All at Once
Year: 2022
Directors: Daniel Scheinert, Daniel Kwan

This movie is so beautiful. Every actor is amazing, every scene pulls you in closer. I love this movie becuase of the mundane elements that are given so much value, like the everything bagel.

The Virgin Suicides
 Year: 2000
Director: Sofia Coppola

 Such a monumental movie to my development as a young girl. Reading the book and then watching the movie enlightened me to the nuance of the male gaze within media, and everyday life. Also I love Sofia Coppola
